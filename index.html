About

I come from a very abnormal background for a software engineer. I grew up on a ranch in a small town. My two hobbies in life are polar opposites: riding horses and playing video games. I showed my horses as a youth president of my 4-H club, and was captain of my High School Equestrian Drill Team. My motivation in technology stemmed from my love of video games, primarily the Halo franchise. I dream of giving back to the community that gave me so much joy throughout my childhood.


Over the years, these have transformed from extracurricular activities to full on careers. I’ve been supporting my girlfriend of 4 years with her horse training business while I’ve been becoming the best game programmer I can be. Now the time has come for me to seek gainful employment, and if you have been directed this page, then I sincerely hope you know your consideration of me is not misplaced.

Generalist Programmer & Game Developer.

I enjoy making things work. It’s as simple as that. I get a huge bonus of enjoyment when the things I make work lead to enjoyment of others, hence I chose to join the games industry. The team I’m working with and the project I’m working on is far more important than the system I’m working in. I have worked my entire college carrier to have an understanding of all parts of the game engine and beyond so that I may be employed to work on any part of it. Whether it be AI, gameplay, graphics, physics, tools, you name it, I can help make it work.
