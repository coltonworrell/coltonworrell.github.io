About

I come from a very abnormal background for a software engineer. I grew up on a ranch in a small town. My two hobbies in life are polar opposites: riding horses and playing video games. Over the years, these have transformed from extracurricular activities to full on careers. I’ve been supporting my girlfriend of 4 years with her horse training business while I’ve been becoming the best game programmer I can be. Now the time has come for me to seek gainful employment, and if you have been directed this page, then I sincerely hope you know your consideration of me is not misplaced.

Generalist Programmer & Game Developer.

I enjoy making things work. It’s as simple as that. I get a huge bonus of enjoyment when the things I make work lead to enjoyment of others, hence I chose to join the games industry. I have worked my entire college carrier to have an understanding of all parts of the game engine and beyond so that I may be employed to work on any part of it. Whether it be AI, gameplay, graphics, physics, tools, you name it, I can help make it work.
